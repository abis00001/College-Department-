Continuous Internal Assessment (CIA)

The Continuous Internal Assessment (CIA) system is designed to evaluate the academic performance of students throughout the semester. The assessment is divided into various components, ensuring a comprehensive evaluation of a student's progress.

CIA Components

  • Internal Tests: Regular tests conducted during the semester to assess students' understanding of the subjects.
  • Assignments: Assignments given to evaluate the students' analytical and research skills.
  • Project Work: Practical project work to help students apply theoretical knowledge.
  • Class Participation: Active participation in class discussions and activities is also considered for assessment.
  • Presentations: Students are required to present their projects or topics during the semester to improve their communication skills.
