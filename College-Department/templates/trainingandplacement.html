Training and Placement

The Training and Placement Cell at St. Joseph's College focuses on providing ample opportunities for students to excel in their careers. We ensure that our students receive extensive training in both technical and soft skills to prepare them for various industries.

Objectives of the Training and Placement Cell

  • Provide training to students to enhance their employability skills.
  • Arrange on-campus recruitment drives by reputed companies.
  • Offer career counseling and job preparation workshops.
  • Assist students in developing communication, teamwork, and leadership skills.

Placement Highlights

In the past years, our department has successfully placed numerous students in top-tier companies. Some of the major recruiters include:

  • Google
  • Microsoft
  • Amazon
  • TCS
  • Infosys
  • Wipro
  • Accenture
  • IBM

Training Programs

We offer the following training programs to ensure that our students are industry-ready:

  • Pre-Placement Training (PPT)
  • Personality Development Programs
  • Mock Interviews
  • Group Discussions
  • Industry-Specific Workshops

Placement Process

Our placement process follows a structured approach:

  1. Pre-placement Talk: Companies give an overview of their organization and job profiles.
  2. Written Test: Many companies conduct online/offline written tests for shortlisting candidates.
  3. Technical Interviews: After passing the written test, students undergo technical interviews based on their respective domains.
  4. HR Interviews: Candidates who clear the technical rounds face the HR round for personality and behavioral assessments.
