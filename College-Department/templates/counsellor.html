Counsellor

The Department of Computer Science offers counseling services to help students navigate personal, academic, and emotional challenges. Our experienced counselors are available to provide guidance and support to students in a safe and confidential environment.

Services Offered

  • Academic Counseling: Helping students with academic stress, study strategies, and time management.
  • Personal Counseling: Assisting students in managing personal issues such as anxiety, relationships, and life balance.
  • Career Counseling: Providing career advice, including guidance on internships, job opportunities, and career paths in the tech industry.
  • Mental Health Support: Offering resources and support for students dealing with mental health concerns.

How to Make an Appointment

Students can make an appointment with the counselor through the department office or by filling out the online appointment form. The counseling service is available during regular working hours, and urgent appointments can be scheduled as needed.
