Scholarships

At the Department of Computer Science, we believe in making education accessible to everyone. We offer a range of scholarships to support deserving students in their academic journey. Below are the details of the scholarships available:

  • Merit-Based Scholarship

    Offered to students who demonstrate outstanding academic performance. This scholarship covers up to 50% of tuition fees.

  • Need-Based Scholarship

    Available for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. The scholarship is based on family income and academic performance.

  • Sports Scholarship

    This scholarship is offered to students who have excelled in sports and represent the college at the inter-collegiate level.

  • Research Scholarship

    Aimed at students pursuing research projects or contributing to publications. It provides financial assistance for research expenses and resources.

How to Apply

Interested candidates can apply by visiting the Department office or by submitting their application through our online portal. Please ensure to attach all necessary documents, including academic transcripts, proof of income (for need-based scholarships), and a letter of recommendation.
