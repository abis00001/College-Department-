Office of the Controller of Examinations (COE)

The Office of the Controller of Examinations (COE) at St. Joseph's College is responsible for managing all examination-related activities. The office ensures that the examination process is carried out in an orderly manner, adhering to the prescribed guidelines.

Functions of the COE

  • Examination Schedule: Preparation and publication of the examination schedule for all courses.
  • Admit Cards: Issuing admit cards for students to appear in examinations.
  • Examination Conduct: Supervising the examination process, invigilation, and ensuring exam integrity.
  • Result Declaration: Timely declaration of results and preparation of mark sheets.
  • Revaluation & Supplementary Examinations: Managing revaluation requests and supplementary exams.

Examination Rules and Regulations

Students must adhere to the following rules and regulations during their examinations:

  • Identity Verification: Students must carry their ID card and admit card during the examination.
  • No Electronic Devices: Mobile phones and other electronic devices are prohibited in the exam hall.
  • Academic Integrity: Cheating or malpractice will lead to severe penalties.
  • Attendance Criteria: Students must meet the required attendance to be eligible for exams.

Important Dates

  • End Date for Exam Registration: April 15, 2024
  • Revaluation Request Deadline: June 10, 2024
