Joseph's College is an affiliated First Grade College of the Bharathidasan University.
It was established in 1844 by the Fathers of Society of Jesus (The Jesuits).
It was affiliated to the then Madras University in 1866.
The College celebrated its centenary in 1944 and sesquicentenary in 1995.

About Us

The Department of Computer Science at St. Joseph's College is committed to excellence in education and fostering innovation. Our courses equip students with essential skills to thrive in the competitive global market, backed by a strong focus on research and development.
