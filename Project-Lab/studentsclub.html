Student Club

The Student Club at the Department of Computer Science offers various opportunities for students to enhance their skills, build teamwork, and engage in extracurricular activities. The club organizes events, workshops, hackathons, and more to help students develop both academically and socially.

Key Activities

  • Workshops: Hands-on sessions on the latest technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Web Development, and Cybersecurity.
  • Hackathons: Coding competitions and challenges to boost problem-solving skills and encourage collaboration.
  • Guest Lectures: Talks by industry experts to give insights into real-world applications of computer science.
  • Social Events: Get-togethers and social activities to build a sense of community among students.
